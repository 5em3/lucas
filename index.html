Easy Crepes Recipe

‚è≥ Preparation time: 15 minutes | Servings: people

Ingredients:

  • 50g flour
  • 1 egg
  • 75ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 5g melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, beat eggs, add milk and melted butter.
  3. Slowly mix everything together until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan on medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour a little batter, spread thinly.
  6. Cook 1-2 minutes per side until golden.
  7. Eat with toppings you like!
